Empowering Underprivileged Students for a Brighter Future

About Us

Abhyudaya is a transformative mentorship program initiated by SPJIMR. Our focus is to provide holistic after-school education and mentorship to talented yet underprivileged children, whom we call "Sitaras" (meaning "stars"). The program empowers these children with academic guidance, life skills, and opportunities to grow into confident individuals ready to shape their future.

We strongly align with the **United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 4 - Quality Education and SDG 17 - Partnerships for the Goals)** to ensure every child has access to quality education and mentorship that supports their long-term growth.

Our Mission

Through our structured mentorship approach, we aim to bridge the opportunity gap for underprivileged students. Each first-year PGDM student at SPJIMR is assigned a Sitara for a year-long mentorship program, involving home visits, career counseling, and personal development sessions.

Beyond academics, Abhyudaya promotes physical and emotional well-being, sports engagement, and vocational training to help children achieve independence and success.

Our Impact

Since its inception in 2008, Abhyudaya has positively impacted over **5,000 children**, supporting them in academics, career aspirations, and overall personal development.

Our success stories include children securing scholarships, participating in national-level sports, and becoming first-generation graduates. Annual events like **Hastantaran** (mentor handover ceremony) and **Green Games** (sports event) further strengthen mentor-mentee relationships and community engagement.
